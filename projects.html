Point of Sale and Inventory System for Ephraim's Shop

A point of sale and inventory system is a software-based business solution for tracking sales and inventories at the same time.

Limitation

  1. User Information
    • The admin can search and retrieve the user`s information. Also he/she can add and update the user’s information if needed. And he/she can view his information. The user can also update his or her password if necessary.
  2. Point-of-Sale System
    • This system helps make transactions faster.
    • The system can help the store be more productive.
    • The system lessens the miscalculation and to be more accurate.
    • The system helps you improve your operations for your customers.
  3. Inventory
    • Adding a new product to inventory
    • Editing or updating the product price
    • Deleting a product to the inventory
    • Searching/Retrieving a product to the inventory
    • Can see/view the remaining stocks of every product.
    • Reduce the Risk of Out-Of-Stock Problems
    • Simplify Inventory Management
    • Accurately Forecast Future Sales
    • The system is using SQL Database

RECOMMENDATION

Online Reservation for the item that is wanted by customers but not in the store yet. The System doesn’t have a discount on every item/product yet. The system doesn’t have an online payment, it makes the system more features. The system is not able to get information on the internet, it makes the system easier-to-use. The system doesn't cover Refund process

Language Used

  • Java(Swing Components)
  • MySQL

Barangay Information System for Barangay San Agustin

The general objective of the study is to create a system that is fast, accurate and effective method in keeping useful records. It helps to minimize the time in writing the records of the residents.

Limitation

  1. Register Residence Information
    • This system can only store personal information; there will be a brief interview before the information is saved in the system. Name, address, gender, and other requirements are some of the information that may be required. This is just for residents of the barangay.
  2. Request Barangay Permit
    • A menu bar will be available in the system for requesting a barangay permit and certificate. A schedule is required when requesting a permit and certificate. Only those who live in the barangay can request this form, and it can only process 10 to 15 persons each day.
  3. Create Barangay Programs And Projects
    • Only the barangay's administration and staff have access to this system, and only the residents of the barangay will be assisted. This technology is only for broadcasting programs and projects.
  4. Generate Statistical Reports
    • This system, which is generated only by the administration and employees, keeps track of the population in the entire barangay in order to provide adequate supply for the residents. This system is updated as the number of residents grows.

RECOMMENDATION

The proposed system has its some limitations that must be taken into consideration: (1) The system is controlled by pull-up buttons, which allow the user to quickly enter the menu and select from a variety of menus with various functions based on his or her preferences, (2) Bugs in the proposed system cannot be avoided, (3) The system administrator should be the one responsible in backing-up the files and restoring them when bugs interfere in the system, (4) Transactions on the system are also limited due to the researchers’ own knowledge of developing the program, (5) All systems always require updates new version and enhancements.

Language Used

  • VB.net(WinForms Application)
  • MS Access
